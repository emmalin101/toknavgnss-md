# TOKNAV SEO / GEO / AIO Blog Topic Plan

## Research Basis

Business context: TOKNAV is positioned as a GNSS receiver manufacturer and high-precision positioning solution provider for overseas B2B buyers, including importers, distributors, surveying and mapping companies, system integrators, engineering contractors, EPC companies, precision agriculture dealers and OEM/ODM partners.

Product context: GNSS RTK receivers, portable RTK receivers, CORS/base station receivers, GNSS antennas, rugged GIS data collectors, precision agriculture auto-steering systems, machine control systems, VRS/CORS services, deformation monitoring and line marking robot solutions.

Google SEO / AIO direction: The topics are designed for pages that answer concrete buyer questions clearly enough for Google organic search, AI Overviews and answer engines. The content avoids keyword stuffing and uses procurement, configuration, testing, application and supplier evaluation language.

## Topic 1

**Blog Title:** How to Choose an RTK GNSS Receiver for Surveying, Mapping and Construction Projects

**Target Keywords:**
- RTK GNSS receiver
- GNSS receiver for surveying
- RTK receiver supplier
- how to choose GNSS receiver
- GNSS RTK receiver manufacturer

**Search Intent:** Commercial investigation / procurement decision. The searcher is comparing receiver specifications, field requirements, supplier capability and quotation factors before contacting a manufacturer or distributor.

**Why It Fits the Blog:** This topic sits very close to buyer decision-making. It can attract distributors, contractors and survey teams who are not ready to ask for price yet but need a practical buying framework. It also supports internal links to GNSS receiver category pages, product comparison pages and the inquiry form.

## Topic 2

**Blog Title:** CORS/VRS vs Base-Rover RTK: Which GNSS Correction Setup Is Right for Your Project?

**Target Keywords:**
- CORS VRS service
- GNSS correction service
- base rover RTK
- CORS GNSS receiver
- network RTK solution

**Search Intent:** Solution evaluation / technical comparison. The searcher wants to understand whether to use a single base-rover workflow, a CORS network, a VRS service or a hybrid deployment.

**Why It Fits the Blog:** This topic supports higher-value system integration inquiries, especially from organizations planning infrastructure, monitoring, surveying networks, agriculture operations or regional correction services. It is also a good AI Overview topic because it answers a comparison-style question with clear definitions and decision criteria.

## Topic 3

**Blog Title:** GNSS Auto-Steering and Machine Control: A Practical Buying Guide for Precision Agriculture Dealers

**Target Keywords:**
- GNSS auto steering system
- precision agriculture GNSS
- farm autosteer system
- machine control GNSS
- GNSS land leveling system

**Search Intent:** Application and solution procurement. The searcher is evaluating GNSS-based systems for agriculture machinery, land leveling, dozer guidance or dealer distribution.

**Why It Fits the Blog:** Precision agriculture and machine control are high-value solution pages where buyers need installation preparation, compatibility checks, accuracy expectations and after-sales support. This topic can generate better leads than broad educational content because it speaks to distributors and project buyers.
